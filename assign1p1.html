Biography

Profile

As head of House Lannister, he is the Lord of Casterly Rock, the capital of his realm, known as the Westerlands. He is also a former King's Hand, the current Warden of the West, and father of Queen Cersei.

Reputation

Tywin is most known for his cunning nature and ruthlessness against his enemies. As a young man, he led his forces against a rebellion by House Reyne, one of his vassal houses. The Reyens were utterly destroyed and thier holdings razed to the ground. As his son Tyrion put it, "Seven kingdoms unite in fear of Tywin Lannister."

Chracter

Tywin's greatest personal ambition is not in glory, or in honor, as was common among his peers. Rather, he held firm to his belief that it is everyone's duty to do their best to improve the status of their house. Ironically, he has a poor relationship with all 3 of his children.

Reception

Played by British actor Charles Dance, Tywin was a fan favorite and was known to dominate his scenes. Many fanmade artpieces, music videos, and the such have been made in honor of this character.
